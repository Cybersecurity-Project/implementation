This is a simple and basic example of using Homomorphic Encryption to determine if Siri has a higher salary than Alexa. If Alexa has a higher salary, then it will return a 1, and 0 otherwise. The salaries of Siri and Alexa will be defined in two bit binary values:
00 (Less than 50,000)
01 (Between 50,000 and 150,000)
10 (Between 160,000 and 250,000)
11 (Higher than 250,000)
